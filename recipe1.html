Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 300g spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and minced garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes and red wine (if using), stir well.
  4. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain.
  6. Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti, garnish with Parmesan cheese.
