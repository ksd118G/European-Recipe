Moussaka

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced
  • 1 pound ground lamb or beef
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place sliced eggplants on a baking sheet, brush with olive oil, and bake until golden brown (about 15-20 minutes).
  3. In a pan, brown ground lamb or beef over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, red wine, oregano, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to create a roux. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth. Add nutmeg and cook until thickened.
  6. Layer half of the eggplant slices in a baking dish. Top with the meat sauce, then the remaining eggplant slices.
  7. Pour the béchamel sauce over the eggplant and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and breadcrumbs.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  9. Allow to cool for a few minutes before serving. Slice and enjoy your Moussaka!
