Borscht

Ingredients:

  • 3 beets, peeled and shredded
  • 2 carrots, peeled and shredded
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small cabbage, shredded
  • 1 can (14 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream and fresh dill for serving

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  2. Add shredded beets, carrots, and diced potatoes to the pot. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in shredded cabbage, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, and tomato paste. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in beef or vegetable broth, add bay leaves, and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20-25 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in red wine vinegar and adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Remove bay leaves before serving.
  7. Serve Borscht hot, topped with a dollop of sour cream and fresh dill.
