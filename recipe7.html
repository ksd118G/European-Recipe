Goulash

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream and chopped parsley for serving

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Season the beef cubes with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Brown the beef cubes on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add chopped onions and cook until softened. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Stir in sweet paprika, caraway seeds, dried thyme, bay leaf, and tomato paste. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Return the browned beef to the pot. Pour in beef broth and red wine. Bring to a simmer, then cover and cook over low heat for 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is tender.
  6. Add diced potatoes to the pot and continue to simmer until the potatoes are cooked through.
  7. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Remove the bay leaf before serving.
  8. Serve Goulash hot, garnished with a dollop of sour cream and chopped parsley.
