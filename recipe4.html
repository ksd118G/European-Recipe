Fish and Chips

Ingredients:

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into thick strips
  • 4 pieces of white fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200째F (95째C) to keep the cooked items warm while frying.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, cold water, salt, and pepper to make the batter.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large, deep pan to 375째F (190째C).
  4. Dip the fish fillets into the batter, allowing excess to drip off, and carefully place them in the hot oil. Fry until golden brown and crispy (about 5-7 minutes per side).
  5. While the fish is frying, cook the potato strips in batches until golden and crispy (about 4-6 minutes per batch).
  6. Remove the fish and chips from the oil using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  7. Keep the cooked fish and chips warm in the preheated oven while frying the remaining batches.
  8. Serve the Fish and Chips hot with lemon wedges and your favorite dipping sauce.
